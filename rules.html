Café Rules & Guidelines 📋

Keep our cats happy and safe. A few simple guidelines for everyone.

🐾

House Rules – Do's & Don'ts

Do's ✓
Be gentle and let the cats come to you
Stroke them softly on the head and back
Take as many photos as you like!
Use the provided hand sanitiser
Sit and relax – let the magic happen
Tell us if a cat seems unwell or stressed
Ask staff for advice on cat handling
Don'ts ✗
Never pick up a cat without permission
Don't disturb a sleeping cat
No loud noises or running about
Don't restrain or chase the cats
No flash photography directly in their eyes
Don't feed them your food or drinks
No children under 4 without adult supervision

Our Adoption Process

Giving our cats their forever homes

🐾
1
Choose Your Cat

Visit our café and spend time with the cats. Get to know their personalities and see if you have a connection.

2
Application Form

Complete our adoption application. We ask about your home, lifestyle, and experience with cats to ensure the best match.

3
Home Visit

Our team visits your home to check it's safe and suitable for your chosen cat. We provide adoption advice.

4
Meet & Greet

We bring the cat to your home for a meet and greet. This helps them settle and you can prepare properly.

5
Adoption Complete

Cat moves in! You'll get our aftercare support and can contact us anytime with questions or updates.

🐾 Adoption fee: £80 (includes microchipping, neutering, vaccination, insurance). All our cats are health-checked and fully prepared for their new homes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about your visit

What if I'm allergic to cats? 🤧
We can't offer a hypoallergenic guarantee, but our café has good air filtration. We also offer alternative options – ask staff about our "Cats from a Distance" package where you can observe and photo without direct contact. You can also book outside visiting hours for a private cat-free tour.
Can I bring my children? 👶
Children under 4 can visit if supervised by a paying adult. Children aged 4–12 are charged £8. Children must be gentle with the cats and follow our guidelines at all times. Teenagers 13+ are charged adult price (£12). Parents are responsible for ensuring their children follow café rules.
What if a cat bites or scratches me? 🐱
Cat bites and scratches are rare at our café. Our cats are hand-selected rescue cats trained to be comfortable with visitors. If a scratch does occur, we have first aid supplies. Please report any incident to staff immediately. You accept the minimal risk of cat interaction when visiting.
Can I cancel or reschedule my booking? 📅
Cancellations are free up to 48 hours before your session. Cancellations within 48 hours forfeit your payment. Rescheduling is free and can be done up to 7 days in advance. Email us at hello@themeowlounge.co.uk or call 0161 234 0000.
Are there facilities for disabled visitors? ♿
Our café has wheelchair access at street level. We have accessible bathrooms and plenty of seating. Service animals (guide dogs) are welcome. If you have specific accessibility needs, please contact us before your visit so we can make appropriate arrangements.
What if I want to adopt a cat? 💛
Many of our cats are available for adoption! Our adoption process includes: 1) Meeting the cat in café 2) Adoption application form 3) Home visit by staff 4) Meet & greet at home 5) Adoption finalised. The process typically takes 2–3 weeks. Ask staff during your visit for more details.
